Hi, I'm a Ph.D. student interested in applying natural language processing and machine learning to study the analysis and synthesis of argumentation (the action or process of systematic reasoning in support of an idea, action, or theory).

Background

I obtained a B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Business Information Systems from Paderborn University. Simultaneously, I worked as a student assistant in the Computational Social Science group on the effect of people's beliefs and morals in argumentation.

Research

During my doctorate, I specialize in objectifying arguments. That is, presenting arguments in such a way that their critical evaluation is supported by good communication while avoiding anything rude, disrespectful, or otherwise likely to cause someone to leave a discussion.
