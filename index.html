Hi, I'm a Ph.D. student interested in applying natural language processing and machine learning to study the analysis and synthesis of argumentation (the action or process of systematic reasoning in support of an idea, action, or theory).

Background
I obtained a BA and MA in Business Information Systems from Paderborn University. Subsequently, I worked as a research assistant in the Computational Social Science group on the effect of people's beliefs and morals in argumentation.
Research
During my doctorate, I specialize in the objectification of arguments. That is to try to present arguments in a way that supports a critical evaluation of an argument through good communication and to avoid anything rude, disrespectful, or otherwise likely to make someone leave a discussion.
